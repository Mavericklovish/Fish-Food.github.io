Project Overview

Welcome to the Fish Feeding Simulation! In this interactive web experience, you'll dive into a virtual underwater world
teeming with colorful fishes. Your role is to provide nourishment to these aquatic inhabitants by dropping food into the water.
Watch as the fishes gracefully swim towards the food, eagerly gobbling it up.

  • How to Feed?

  • Click to Feed : Simply click anywhere on the screen to drop food into the water.
  • Watch the Fishes : Observe as the fishes detect the nearest food and swim towards it.
  • Enjoy the Spectacle : Sit back, relax, and enjoy sight of fishes eating food you dropped
    • in water to satisfy their hunger.

Source of Inspiration

  • Nature documentaries showcasing underwater ecosystems and the behavior of aquatic creatures could have inspired the visual design and realism of the fish and their interactions with food item
  • Artwork and illustrations depicting marine life, underwater scenes, and fantasy creatures could have served as inspiration for the visual design and aesthetics of the fish characters and food items.
  • As we delve into the underwater world, we're captivated by the mesmerizing behavior of fishes as they search for food, interact with their environment, and exhibit unique feeding patterns.
  • Our goal is to recreate the beauty and complexity of aquatic life through interactive graphics, allowing users to connect with nature in a virtual environment and gain a deeper appreciation for the delicate harmony of underwater ecosystems.

Objectives

  • Following were our objectives at each step when building this project :

  • To develop a user-friendly interface using HTML5 canvas.
  • Implement interactive functionality so that users can add food at the clicked location.
  • Create a visually appealing fish character that moves.
  • Design food items with varying appearances to add visual interest and realism to the scene.
  • Program the fish to detect the nearest food item and move towards it fluidly.
  • Document the codebase with clear comments and explanations to facilitate future maintenance and extension of the project.

  • Following are the learnings from this project :

  • Canvas drawing : Understanding how to use the HTML5 canvas element and drawing basic shapes.
  • Event handling : Implementing event listeners to detect user input, such as mouse clicks.
  • Animations and Transformations : Creating smooth animations using requestAnimationFrame and updating canvas elements dynamically.
  • Food eating : Implementing the logic for fish to detect nearest food and eat it.

Challenges

    Challenge 1 : Due to less experience in JavaScript language while at the start of making the project it was difficult to do some tasks and move ahead to make the project.

    Challenge 2 : Adding addEventListener for mouse click such that the food appears on the screen when you click.

    Challenge 3 : Achieving smooth rendering and animation performance on the HTML5 canvas.

    Challenge 4 : Designing an intuitive and user-friendly interface for adding food items and interacting with the simulation

Overcoming Challenges

  • Break tasks into smaller steps, learn concepts as you progress using online resources, and regularly practice coding to reinforce skills.
  • Experimented with small code snippets to test and refine our implementation until we reached the desired functionality.
  • Simplify complex calculations, such as collision detection to minimize computational overhead. Use efficient algorithms and data structures to optimize performance.
  • Create prototypes to visualize and test different interface layouts, interactions, and functionalities before implementing them in the final project.
